My name is Taylor Noah. 

I’m a senior software engineer with experience across frontend and backend systems, specializing in distributed architectures and building reliable, scalable backend services. I'm experienced in test-driven and object oriented practices, but am always open to learning different paradigms suited for different needs.

My work has primarily focused on designing APIs, event‑driven systems, and backend platforms able to support high-volume traffic. I'm highly capable in identifying areas of improvement and designing proof-of-concepts that address and improve those areas. 

I approach software holistically. I look at the whole system to better understand how my piece fits in. This helps me make pragmatic decisions around time, scope, and extensibility, and to write code that’s maintainable, testable, and aligned with long‑term needs.

Over the years I’ve contributed across the stack from backend services and data pipelines to frontend features and internal tooling. But my strongest impact comes from backend system design, performance improvements, and simplifying complex architectures.

Additionally, I'm able to seemlessly work across the stack; often being the team expert that bridges knowledge gaps across these domains.
